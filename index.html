Scorching Skies Drink

Spice Up Summer With The Scorching Skies Drink!

The release of 2023 introduced Starbucks lovers to the Summer Berry refresher, a refresher containing the infamous berry delights of Starbucks with a delicious-twist - raspberry boba, and an extraordinary thick bubble tea straw to drink and enjoy the raspberry pearls from. With that addition, the Summer Skies drink - mixed with coconut milk and summer berry refresher base, was easily a big hit from coconut lovers and lemonade haters. But what if I told you, with the addition of Spicy Chili, you could spice up your summer just a little bit more?

Brought to you by the creators of the Sunset Drink - the Scorching Skies Drink offers the perfect mix between hot and intense, and smooth and cozy. Made with Pineapple Passionfruit Base, Spicy Chili Powder, and a Summer Skies Drink - get ready to experience the fury of Summer's heat!

Without further ado, let's get into the mix!

What You'll Need:

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup of Blue Raspberry Concentrate
  • 1/2 cup of Pineapple Passionfruit Concentrate (or pineapple juice with Passionfruit Syrup)
  • 1 tsp Spicy Chili Powder Blend
  • Raspberry Flavoured Pearls
  • 1/2 cup of Coconut Milk
  • Ice Cubes
  • OPTIONAL: Mint Leaves for Garnish
  • OPTIONAL: Vodka

Ordering The Drink At Starbucks

Order a Summer Skies Drink, with Pineapple Passionfruit Base and Spicy Chili Powder Blend. Make sure to confirm the Barista does an 'Ask Me' and writes down: "Shake Pineapple With Chili & Pour On Top".
